OUR STORY

THE DREAMERS

Nash Finley and George McDonough are two dudes who love to skate and design. After receiving a degree in Industrial Design, they were both frustrated with the idea of having to designing products for a company where they would have little to no control on how a something would be produced, manufactured and sold. So they set off to create a design studio where they could control all aspects of the design process and make everything as sustainable as possible. Here they began to prototype and conceptualize products to bring to the market. After countless sleepless nights and designing in the attic of a local woodshop, a skateboard was born made of reclaimed bourbon barrel staves. Since then they have made it their mission to repurpose, reuse, and upcycle. This is Hepcat.

THE HEPCAT TEAM

Hepcat is an upcycled subculture brought back from the 1940s that has been re-imagined and re-designed. The company is the love child of industrial design, sustainability, and of course skateboarding. The team shares the same passion to inspire and re-think the way they use materials in design. They are a collective of skateboarders, designers, and philosophers reshaping the world, starting with what they know best.
